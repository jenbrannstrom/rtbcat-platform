# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect all participants to be respectful, constructive, and professional.
Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, doxxing, and abusive behavior are not allowed.

## Expected Behavior

1. Be respectful in all interactions.
2. Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
3. Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
4. Accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. Harassment, threats, or hateful language.
2. Personal attacks or sustained disruptive behavior.
3. Sharing private information without permission.
4. Deliberate sabotage of project discussion or collaboration.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or ban users for violations.
Serious or repeated violations may result in permanent exclusion.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to project maintainers.
Do not post personal details publicly.
